Metrics & Roadmap (1 Page)

Success Metrics (North-Star + Operating)

  1. Screening Time Reduction: Reduce average first-pass screening effort per role by at least 60%.
  2. Time-to-Shortlist: Cut time from application intake to shortlist creation from 3-5 days to under 24 hours.
  3. Interview Yield: Maintain shortlist quality with at least 35% of AI-recommended candidates moving to interviews.
  4. Recruiter Trust Score: Achieve >= 80% recruiter agreement with recommended action during pilot.
  5. Candidate Response SLA: Send initial decision/update to 90% of applicants within 48 hours.

Product Roadmap

Phase 1 (Week 1): Core MVP

  • JD + resume ingestion
  • Deterministic weighted scoring and recommendation
  • n8n sort-and-bucket workflow

Phase 2 (Weeks 2-3): Automation + Reliability

  • PDF parsing with OCR fallback
  • Persistent candidate scoring history
  • Bias review checkpoints and override logs

Phase 3 (Weeks 4-6): Integrations + Optimization

  • ATS sync (Greenhouse/Lever webhook adapter)
  • Email + calendar auto-scheduling agent
  • Role-based rubric calibration and A/B score tuning
